Market Research

We are looking to find around five thousand consumers from the farming and chariy communities for our project to start. As we grow that will include the general population. We will be targeting the communities with advertising on common gardening websites and by making deals with small businesses.

Competitors

Our project stands out from others because of its integrated alarm system. This system notifies the owner of the greenhouse when there is a lack of resources, for example water. This is important because nothing else on the market interacts with the owner, and this feature is crucial to the greenhouse. This project is also less expensive and easier to maintain than other products on the market. Our greenhouse can also be set up anywhere regardless of outlet location or hose.
